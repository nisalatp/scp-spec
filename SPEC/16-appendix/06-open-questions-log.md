# SCP-SPEC-APP-06: Open Questions Log

## Purpose

This document tracks unresolved questions and decisions pending across the SCP program.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- Open architectural, governance, and operational questions

**Out of Scope**
- Resolved decisions

## Definitions

- Open Question: Unresolved design or policy issue.
- Resolution: Approved answer or decision.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- Open questions MUST be tracked centrally.
- Each question SHOULD have an owner.
- Resolved questions MUST be archived.
- This log MAY be referenced by ADRs.
- This log MUST be reviewed manually on a monthly cadence.
- Open questions MUST be prioritized.

## Edge Cases

- Questions spanning multiple domains.
- Stale questions without owners.

## Audit & Logs

- Updates to this log SHOULD be versioned.
- Resolution timestamps SHOULD be recorded.

## Open Questions

- None.
