# SCP-SPEC-SEC-13: Threat Model

## Purpose

This document identifies and categorizes threats to SCP.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- STRIDE threat categories
- Platform-level threats

**Out of Scope**
- Penetration test reports

## Definitions

- Threat: Potential cause of harm.
- Mitigation: Control reducing risk.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- Threats MUST be periodically reviewed.
- Mitigations SHOULD be documented.
- High-risk threats MUST be addressed.

## Edge Cases

- Insider threats
- Supply chain attacks

## Audit & Logs

- Threat assessments MUST be logged.
