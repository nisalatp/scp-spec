# SCP-SPEC-SEC-14: Privacy and Compliance

## Purpose

This document defines privacy and compliance requirements for SCP.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- Data protection principles
- Regulatory compliance

**Out of Scope**
- Legal advice

## Definitions

- PII: Personally identifiable information.
- Data Subject: Individual whose data is processed.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- SCP MUST support data minimization.
- Data residency MUST be enforced per tenant.
- Data subjects SHOULD have transparency rights.
- Deletion MAY be restricted by law.
- Applicable regulations MUST be defined per tenant.

## Edge Cases

- Conflicting legal requirements
- Retention vs right to erasure

## Audit & Logs

- Compliance actions MUST be logged.

- None.
