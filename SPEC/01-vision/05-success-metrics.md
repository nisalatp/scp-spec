# SCP-SPEC-VISION-05: Success Metrics

## Purpose

This document defines how success is measured for SCP at platform, tenant, and vertical levels, emphasizing outcomes, governance quality, and trust.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- Platform adoption and reliability
- Governance effectiveness
- Data quality and consent compliance
- Stakeholder trust and transparency

**Out of Scope**
- Measuring success of individual organizations
- Political or ideological impact assessment

## Definitions

- Metric: A quantitative or qualitative measure.
- KPI: A key performance indicator tied to objectives.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- Success metrics MUST include governance and compliance indicators.
- Metrics MUST be auditable and reproducible.
- Vertical KPIs SHOULD reconcile at umbrella level.
- Tenants MAY define additional local metrics.
- Qualitative outcomes MUST be captured using standardized narratives and rubrics aligned with international best practices, configurable per tenant.
- KPI dispute escalation paths MUST be defined per tenant policy.

## Edge Cases

- High usage with low trust indicators
- Conflicting KPI interpretations across stakeholders

## Audit & Logs

- Metric definitions and calculations MUST be logged.
- Historical metrics MUST remain immutable.
