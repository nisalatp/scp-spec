# SCP-SPEC-GOV-01: Governance Overview

## Purpose

This document defines the overall governance model of the Smart Community Platform (SCP). It establishes how authority, accountability, and control are exercised across tenants, verticals, programs, data, and operations.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- Governance principles and structure
- Roles of umbrella authority, tenants, and verticals
- Enforcement mechanisms

**Out of Scope**
- Technical implementation details
- Legal contracts external to SCP

## Definitions

- Governance: The system of rules and processes by which SCP is directed and controlled.
- Umbrella Authority: The highest governing body overseeing SCP within and across tenants.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- Governance MUST be explicit, documented, and enforceable.
- Governance MUST prioritize consent, transparency, and accountability.
- No action SHOULD bypass defined governance workflows.
- Governance MAY be extended with tenant-specific rules, but not weakened.
- Governance role rotation and review MUST follow internationally accepted standards and tenant policy.
- External observers MUST NOT participate in governance processes by default.

## Edge Cases

- Emergency actions requiring expedited governance.
- Conflicting governance rules between tenants.

## Audit & Logs

- All governance decisions MUST be logged.
- Logs MUST be immutable and reviewable.
