# SCP-SPEC-ARCH-09: Architecture Decisions and ADRs

## Purpose

This document records significant architectural decisions and their rationale to ensure transparency and long-term maintainability.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- Major architectural decisions
- Trade-offs and rejected alternatives

**Out of Scope**
- Minor implementation details
- Temporary experimental changes

## Definitions

- ADR: Architecture Decision Record.
- Decision Owner: Entity responsible for the decision.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- All major decisions MUST be recorded as ADRs.
- ADRs SHOULD include context and consequences.
- Superseded ADRs MUST remain archived.
- ADR templates MAY be standardized.
- ADR approval authority MUST be configurable at the country level.
- ADRs MUST be communicated via internal portal updates and email notifications.

## Edge Cases

- Emergency decisions made under time pressure.
- Conflicting ADRs across time.

## Audit & Logs

- ADR changes MUST be version-controlled.
- Approval history SHOULD be retained.
