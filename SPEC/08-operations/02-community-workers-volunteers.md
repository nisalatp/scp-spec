# SCP-SPEC-OPS-02: Community Workers and Volunteers

## Purpose

This document defines how community workers and volunteers participate in SCP operations with limited and governed access.

## In Scope / Out of Scope

**In Scope**
- Volunteer roles
- Task-based access
- Supervision requirements

**Out of Scope**
- Volunteer recruitment processes
- External volunteer platforms

## Definitions

- Community Worker: Local support personnel.
- Volunteer: Non-staff contributor with restricted access.

## Rules (MUST/SHOULD/MAY)

- Volunteers MUST have restricted, time-bound access.
- Volunteers MUST operate under supervision.
- Volunteers SHOULD have task-specific permissions.
- Sensitive data MUST be masked for volunteers.
- Volunteers MUST complete background checks before access is granted.
- Volunteers MAY become agents through the standard onboarding and approval process.

## Edge Cases

- Volunteer access expiring mid-task.
- Volunteers assisting across multiple geos.

## Audit & Logs

- Volunteer actions MUST be logged.
- Access grants and revocations MUST be auditable.
