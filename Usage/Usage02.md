# Smart Community Platform (SCP) – Cross-Vertical Advanced Query Scenarios (Set 3)

The following scenarios represent **high-complexity SCP queries** that cut across **multiple verticals** (Health, Education, Social Welfare, Housing, Employment, Environment, Governance) and use **5 or more parameters** each.  
These are designed to demonstrate SCP’s **real analytical power**, policy safety, and auditability.

Each scenario explicitly states:
- Verticals involved
- Typical parameters combined
- Intended outcome

---

## 1. Women at Risk of Long-Term Socio-Economic Exclusion  
**Query:**  
Find **unmarried women aged 25–35** who:
- Have completed **Bachelor’s degree**
- Are **unemployed for more than 12 months**
- Come from **low-income households**
- Live in **rural or semi-rural areas**
- Have **no active social welfare support**

**Verticals:** Education, Employment, Social Welfare, Geography  
**Outcome:** Target career grants + job placement programs

---

## 2. High-Risk Mothers in Unsafe Living Conditions  
**Query:**  
Identify **pregnant women** who:
- Are flagged as **high-risk medically**
- Live in households **without proper sanitation**
- Have **household income below poverty threshold**
- Reside **more than 10 km from nearest health facility**
- Have **no regular antenatal visits recorded**

**Verticals:** Health, Housing, Infrastructure, Geography  
**Outcome:** Priority medical + housing + transport intervention

---

## 3. Children Facing Multi-Dimensional Poverty  
**Query:**  
Find **children under 12** who:
- Are **school-enrolled but with irregular attendance**
- Belong to **food-insecure households**
- Live in **overcrowded housing**
- Have **parents with unstable income**
- Show **declining academic performance**

**Verticals:** Education, Social Welfare, Housing, Economy  
**Outcome:** Integrated child protection & family support

---

## 4. Elderly with Health, Income, and Isolation Risks  
**Query:**  
Identify **elderly persons (65+)** who:
- Live **alone**
- Have **chronic health conditions**
- Receive **no pension or income support**
- Reside in **high-risk environmental zones**
- Have **no recorded community visits in last 6 months**

**Verticals:** Health, Social Welfare, Housing, Environment  
**Outcome:** Home-based care + income support + monitoring

---

## 5. NEET Youth in High-Risk Households  
**Query:**  
Find **youth aged 18–29** who:
- Are **not in education, employment, or training**
- Come from **single-income households**
- Have **dependents (siblings or parents)**
- Live in **areas with low job density**
- Have **never participated in skills programs**

**Verticals:** Education, Employment, Social Welfare, Geography  
**Outcome:** Skills training + livelihood placement

---

## 6. Disaster-Sensitive Households with Children  
**Query:**  
Locate households that:
- Are in **flood- or landslide-prone areas**
- Have **children under 5**
- Lack **safe water and sanitation**
- Are **below poverty line**
- Have **no disaster preparedness support**

**Verticals:** Environment, Housing, Health, Social Welfare  
**Outcome:** Relocation or resilient housing + WASH

---

## 7. Women-Led Households with Education Potential  
**Query:**  
Find **women-headed households** where:
- Household income is **below sustainability threshold**
- At least one child is **academically high-performing**
- No member has **higher education exposure**
- Housing condition is **adequate**
- No existing **education sponsorship**

**Verticals:** Education, Gender, Economy, Housing  
**Outcome:** Long-term education sponsorship

---

## 8. Chronic Illness + Poverty + Employment Risk  
**Query:**  
Identify individuals who:
- Have **chronic non-communicable diseases**
- Are **primary income earners**
- Work in **informal or unstable employment**
- Belong to **low-income households**
- Have **no health insurance or aid**

**Verticals:** Health, Employment, Economy, Social Welfare  
**Outcome:** Medical + income protection planning

---

## 9. School Dropout Risk Due to Housing Instability  
**Query:**  
Find students who:
- Changed residence **more than twice in 2 years**
- Come from **rented or temporary housing**
- Have **attendance below 80%**
- Belong to **low-income families**
- Show **declining academic results**

**Verticals:** Education, Housing, Social Welfare  
**Outcome:** Stabilization + education continuity support

---

## 10. Infrastructure Investment with Maximum Health Impact  
**Query:**  
Identify areas where:
- Majority of households lack **toilets**
- Report **high waterborne disease incidence**
- Have **high child population density**
- Are **below average income levels**
- Have **no ongoing infrastructure projects**

**Verticals:** Infrastructure, Health, Demographics, Economy  
**Outcome:** Evidence-based sanitation investment

---

## 11. Long-Term Aid Dependency Without Improvement  
**Query:**  
Find households that:
- Received **aid for over 3 years**
- Show **no income improvement**
- Have **working-age adults**
- Live in **economically active regions**
- Have **no skills or employment linkage**

**Verticals:** Social Welfare, Employment, Economy  
**Outcome:** Shift from aid to empowerment programs

---

## 12. Youth with Migration Risk Indicators  
**Query:**  
Identify youth who:
- Are **unemployed graduates**
- Come from **economically stressed households**
- Live in **high outbound-migration regions**
- Have **no local job opportunities**
- Have **applied for foreign work previously**

**Verticals:** Education, Employment, Migration, Economy  
**Outcome:** Local employment & retention strategies

---

## 13. Child Malnutrition with Environmental Factors  
**Query:**  
Find children who:
- Show **underweight or stunting indicators**
- Live in **food-insecure households**
- Have **unsafe water sources**
- Have **low maternal education levels**
- Are in **remote geographic areas**

**Verticals:** Health, Nutrition, Education, Environment  
**Outcome:** Integrated nutrition + water + education support

---

## 14. Women at Risk of Workforce Exit  
**Query:**  
Identify employed women who:
- Are **sole caregivers**
- Have **children under 5**
- Work in **low-wage informal jobs**
- Have **no childcare access**
- Come from **low-income households**

**Verticals:** Employment, Gender, Family, Economy  
**Outcome:** Childcare + employment retention support

---

## 15. Education Investment ROI Optimization  
**Query:**  
Find students who:
- Perform in **top academic percentile**
- Come from **bottom income percentile**
- Have **stable housing**
- Have **supportive family environment**
- Are at **critical transition stage (O/L → A/L, A/L → Degree)**

**Verticals:** Education, Economy, Family  
**Outcome:** High-impact scholarship targeting

---

## 16. Health Access Gap Analysis  
**Query:**  
Identify populations who:
- Have **high disease prevalence**
- Live **far from health facilities**
- Have **low transport access**
- Are **low-income**
- Have **low healthcare utilization records**

**Verticals:** Health, Transport, Geography, Economy  
**Outcome:** Mobile clinics or facility placement

---

## 17. Women with Digital Inclusion Potential  
**Query:**  
Find women who:
- Have **secondary or higher education**
- Are **currently unemployed**
- Own **smartphones**
- Live in **connectivity-available areas**
- Come from **low-income households**

**Verticals:** Education, Technology, Employment, Economy  
**Outcome:** Digital work / remote employment programs

---

## 18. Multi-Generational Poverty Risk  
**Query:**  
Identify families where:
- Grandparents, parents, and children all have **low education**
- Household income is **persistently low**
- No member has **formal employment**
- Children show **early learning delays**
- No external assistance received historically

**Verticals:** Education, Economy, Social Welfare  
**Outcome:** Whole-family intervention strategy

---

## 19. Climate Vulnerability with Economic Fragility  
**Query:**  
Find households that:
- Depend on **climate-sensitive livelihoods**
- Live in **high climate-risk zones**
- Have **no savings or insurance**
- Are **below income threshold**
- Have **school-going children**

**Verticals:** Environment, Economy, Education  
**Outcome:** Resilience + livelihood diversification

---

## 20. Community-Level Compound Risk Index  
**Query:**  
Rank communities based on:
- Poverty rate
- Health risk prevalence
- Education dropout levels
- Infrastructure gaps
- Disaster exposure

**Verticals:** Governance, Health, Education, Infrastructure, Environment  
**Outcome:** Strategic, multi-year development planning

---

## Strategic Takeaway

**These scenarios show SCP is not a registry system — it is a cross-vertical decision intelligence platform capable of ethically answering questions that were previously impossible to ask or justify.**
