# Audit & Logging: Umbrella Authority

## Logged Actions
*   `policy.approve`, `program.approve`
*   `conflict.resolve`
*   `override.apply`

## Visibility
*   Can see all logs related to Governance violations and Conflicts.
*   Decisions must be justifiable in audit trails.

## Correlation
*   Decisions linked to specific `conflict_id` or `program_id`.
