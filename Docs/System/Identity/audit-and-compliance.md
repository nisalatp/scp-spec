# Audit & Compliance: Identity

## Logged Events
- `person.create`
- `person.update` (captures old/new values).
- `person.view` (Access Log - Critical for Privacy).

## Fields
- `purpose` (Why was this viewed?).
- `consent_proof` (Ref to consent ID).

## Retention
- Identity records are **Permanent**. Archived records are never hard-deleted unless legally mandated (Right to be Forgotten).
