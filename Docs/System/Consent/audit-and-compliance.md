# Audit & Compliance: Consent Management

## Logged Events
- `consent.capture`, `consent.verify`, `consent.withdraw`, `consent.reject`.

## Fields
- `reason_code` (Essential for withdrawal).
- `evidence_id` (Link to proof).

## Retention
- Consent Records: **Permanent** (Legal defense).
- Withdrawn records are key to proving "We had permission *at the time*".
